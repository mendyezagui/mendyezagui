About Mayta Malamud, M.A., CCC-SLP

Mayta has over 15 years of experience in pediatric speech therapy. She founded Voice Forward Speech to integrate iPads, specialized apps, and interactive digital tools into therapy—making sessions more engaging, motivating, and tailored to each child’s unique needs.

Why Technology?

Traditional barriers—geography, cost, and scheduling—can limit consistent therapy. Digital tools:

  • Enable engaging, game-based practice that holds attention
  • Provide real-time progress tracking for both families and therapists
  • Allow seamless teletherapy sessions—perfect for busy families

Proven Impact

  • 96% of speech-language pathologists report using telepractice with children aged 7–12—demonstrating broad professional adoption for remote therapy¹.
  • In a review of 9 controlled studies, 89% showed outcomes equal or better via telepractice versus in-person sessions².
  • Among parents whose children received teletherapy, 66% found it “somewhat effective” and 26.4% “very effective”—with only 7.6% rating it ineffective³.
  • Over 55% of U.S. children ages 3–17 with a communication disorder received intervention services in the past year⁴.
  • The global AAC devices market was valued at USD 2.31 B in 2024 and is projected to reach USD 5.23 B by 2031 (CAGR 8.11%)—reflecting growing demand for modern communication aids⁵.
