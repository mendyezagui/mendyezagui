About Mayta Malamud, M.A., CCC-SLP

Mayta has over 15 years of experience in pediatric speech therapy. She specializes in conducting comprehensive AAC assessments, integrating dynamic tools like iPads, and supporting children with all language learning profiles—including those who are Gestalt Language Processors (GLP).

Whether through traditional articulation support or advanced assistive technology, Mayta’s approach is rooted in evidence, compassion, and a belief that every child deserves to be heard.

While most therapists spend their lifetime with specific tools and try to fit your child into the tool they know, Mayta's comprehensive approach allows her team to select the best tool for your child's needs - dramatically increasing likelihood of success for your child.

Why Technology?

Technology isn’t just a convenience—it’s a catalyst for communication. At Voice Forward Speech, we use digital tools to:

  • Engage children through interactive, play-based apps
  • Enable teletherapy that’s secure, flexible, and consistent
  • Track progress with data-rich visuals and real-time feedback
  • Personalize AAC tools with vocabulary that reflects the child’s life

Why It Works: Comprehensive + Personalized Support

Mayta’s strength is seeing the whole child—not just the speech needs. With deep AAC expertise, modern tech integration, and certification in Gestalt Language Processing (GLP), her approach unlocks meaningful growth in children who need customized support.
