About Mayta Malamud

With over 15 years of dedicated experience in pediatric speech therapy, Mayta Malamud is passionate about harnessing the latest technologies to help children find their voice. As the founder of Voice Forward Speech, she integrates iPads, specialized apps, and interactive digital tools into every session—making therapy engaging, motivating, and tailored to each child’s unique needs.

Why Technology Matters

Modern technology overcomes traditional barriers—geography, cost, and access—to provide consistent, high-quality practice opportunities. Interactive apps keep children engaged and motivated, while built-in progress tracking and automated feedback help therapists monitor growth in real time.
